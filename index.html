VR Dodgeball

Toronto Metropolitan University, Computer Science Department

Course: CPS643: Virtual Reality

Project Authors: Jonathan Romao, Kelly Chau


Our VR Dodgeball game is meant to invoke the nostalgia of the classic game in virtual reality. From the starting scene you will be able to choose between two game modes: target practice and game mode. In target practice you will be able to practice your aim by throwing dodgeballs at targets in the environment. In game mode you will be able to play against an npc opponent in the gym.
