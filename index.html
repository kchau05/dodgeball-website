VR Dodgeball

Toronto Metropolitan University, Computer Science Department

Course: CPS643: Virtual Reality

Project Authors: Jonathan Romao, Kelly Chau

Project Description

Our VR Dodgeball game is meant to provide entertainment and invoke the nostalgia of the classic game in virtual reality. From the locker scene you will be able to choose between two game modes: practice mode and play mode.
In practice mode, you will be able to practice your aim by throwing dodgeballs at targets in the environment. When the current level is completed, you will be able to move on to the next level that will have more targets and different movement patterns. In play mode, you will be able to play against NPCs in the gym. The NPCs will throw dodgeballs at you and you will have to hit them all to win the game.
